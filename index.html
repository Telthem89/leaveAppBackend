Welcome to Mutumba High - Leave Application Portal


The Mutumba High School began its operations with three Form 1 classes in 1981 in Madziva. Two classrooms, and what would later become the Primary School library, were used for lessons. Assemblies took place in a tent during the rainy season and out in the open air once the rains had passed. That girl is now a parent of one of our pupils.
